Tips & Tricks

Picking the right font or text style is an important part of the design process for many designers. Legibility, which is the ability to regognize individual charecters in a text, and readablity which is the ease at which one can understand a text, are closely realted to the right chocie of font and fontsize. Things like kerning, and lineheight can be tweeked in an exsiting font to ease both readability and legibilty.
