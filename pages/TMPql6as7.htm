Tips & tricks

This is where you find tips and trick from the business! All the processes of designing a website have a chronological order, but they are repeated sometimes several times as new information and design decision are made during developement. This creates a circular motion, or iterations of the individual processes. A website in not stationary product but an organic entity, which developes and improves over time!
